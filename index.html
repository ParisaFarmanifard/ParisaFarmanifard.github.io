Research

One of my projects involved using Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) for cross-spectral face recognition. This method helped bridge the gap between different spectral images, enhancing face recognition across varied lighting conditions. Currently, my main focus is on exploring the potential of foundation models and large language models (LLMs). I have successfully applied foundation models for precise iris segmentation, which is crucial for increasing the accuracy and efficiency of iris recognition systems. By integrating these cutting-edge models into my research, I aim to expand the capabilities of biometric technology and security solutions.
